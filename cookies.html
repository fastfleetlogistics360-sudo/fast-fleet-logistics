Cookie and Data Consent

How FastFleet remembers your preferences.

Effective May 18, 2026. This notice explains essential browser storage used by FastFleet pages.

Essential storage

FastFleet uses browser storage for login sessions, cookie consent, theme preference, wallet preview state, delivery drafts, saved support tickets, and demo-mode dashboard records.

Service providers

When live services are enabled, Supabase may store authentication/session details and Paystack may process payment references needed for wallet funding, receipts, refunds, and dispute review.

Your choices

You can clear browser storage from your device settings. Removing essential storage may sign you out or reset local delivery drafts, theme settings, and preview records.
