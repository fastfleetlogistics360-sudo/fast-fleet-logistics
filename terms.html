The rules for using FastFleet.

Effective May 17, 2026. These terms apply to customers, riders, business accounts, and visitors using FAST FLEET LOGISTICS.

User agreement

You agree to provide accurate account, pickup, delivery, recipient, and payment information. You are responsible for ensuring packages are lawful, properly packed, and ready for pickup at the stated location.

Rider agreement

Riders must provide accurate KYC information, keep vehicle and payout details current, follow dispatch instructions, protect packages, and comply with applicable road, safety, tax, and logistics rules.

Payments and wallet

FastFleet may support card, transfer, wallet, cash, or business wallet payments. Wallet top-ups are credited after payment verification. Refunds, chargebacks, failed deliveries, and rider withdrawals may require admin review.

Prohibited items

Users may not send illegal goods, weapons, controlled substances, stolen items, hazardous materials, cash bundles, live animals, or any item prohibited by Nigerian law or platform policy. FastFleet may refuse, cancel, or report suspicious deliveries.

Delivery disputes

Delivery disputes should be reported through support with tracking code, recipient details, evidence, and payment reference. FastFleet may review route history, rider notes, support records, and payment status to resolve disputes.

Real-world services and app stores

FastFleet payments are for real-world logistics, courier movement, rider payouts, and delivery services. Platform wallet or payment rules may change if an app store, payment provider, or regulator requires a different treatment.

Account suspension and deletion

FastFleet may suspend accounts involved in fraud, unsafe behavior, false KYC, payment abuse, harassment, or prohibited deliveries. Users may request deletion through the dashboard or support, subject to active delivery, dispute, and legal retention checks.
