Privacy Policy

How FastFleet handles user data.

Effective May 17, 2026. This policy is written for customers, riders, business accounts, and support users of FAST FLEET LOGISTICS.

Data we collect

We collect account details such as name, phone number, email address, account type, delivery addresses, support messages, wallet activity, payment references, and delivery history. Rider onboarding may collect KYC information such as vehicle details, license information, NIN where required, bank payout details, document images, and operating zone.

Location data

Location is used to detect pickup points, estimate routes, match nearby riders, show live tracking, and support delivery proof. Browser location is requested only when a user taps a location feature. Rider location may be used during active dispatch work to update customers and administrators.

Payments and wallets

Wallet top-ups and payment references may be processed through Paystack. We do not store full card numbers. We may store amount, status, provider reference, wallet balance, refund status, withdrawal status, and related metadata needed for receipts, reconciliation, fraud prevention, and dispute handling.

Storage and processors

FastFleet may use Supabase for authentication, database records, file storage, and sessions. We may use Paystack for payments and map providers for route/location display. These providers process data only as needed to provide authentication, storage, payments, support, tracking, and fraud-prevention services.

Cookies and consent

We use local storage and browser session technologies for login sessions, wallet state, consent records, demo-mode data, and app preferences. We disclose Supabase sessions, Paystack payment references, and map/location features through the cookie and data consent notice.

Data rights and deletion

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data through the dashboard deletion control or the support page. We queue account deletion requests and review active deliveries, payment disputes, fraud prevention obligations, and legally required accounting records before final removal.

Contact

For privacy requests, account deletion, or data safety questions, contact FastFleet through support.html or email support@fastfleetlogistics.com.
