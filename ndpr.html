FastFleet data protection statement.

FastFleet is designed around Nigerian data protection expectations for delivery, wallet, rider, and support records.

Lawful and limited use

FastFleet collects personal data for delivery booking, dispatch matching, rider KYC, wallet verification, support, receipts, safety, fraud prevention, and legal or accounting obligations.

Security and retention

Records are kept only as long as needed for active services, disputes, fraud review, tax/accounting requirements, or lawful operational needs. Access should be limited to authorized platform operators and service providers.

User rights

Users may request access, correction, export, restriction, or deletion through the support page. Some records may be retained where required for compliance, payment disputes, or safety reviews.
